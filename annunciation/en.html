Annunciation

Troparion: Today is the crown of our salvation / and the revelation of the mystery which is from before the ages! / The Son of God becometh the Son of the Virgin, / and Gabriel announceth the glad tidings of grace. / Wherefore, with him let us cry out to the Theotokos: / Rejoice, O thou who art full of grace! / The Lord is with thee!

Kontakion: To thee, the champion leader, / we, thy servants, dedicate hymns of victory and thanksgiving, / as ones delivered from evils, O Theotokos; / but in that thou hast invincible might, / free us from all misfortunes, that we may cry to thee: / Rejoice, O Bride unwedded!

Magnification: We cry out to thee with the voice of the archangel, O pure one: / Rejoice, O thou who art full of grace! / The Lord is with thee!
