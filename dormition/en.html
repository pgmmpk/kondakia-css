Dormition

Troparion: In giving birth thou didst preserve thy virginity, / and in thy falling asleep thou hast not forsaken the world, O Theotokos. / Thou hast been translated to life, / as thou art the Mother of Life. / And by thy supplications thou dost deliver our souls from death.

Kontakion: The tomb and mortality could not hold the Theotokos, / who is untiring in her supplications and our certain hope in her intercessions. / For, as the Mother of Life, / she hath passed over to the Life / Who dwelt within her ever-virgin womb.

Magnification: We magnify thee, / O all-immaculate Mother of Christ our God, / and we glorify / thy most glorious dormition.
