The Holy Pascha

Troparion: Christ is risen from the dead, / trampling down death by death, / and upon those in the tombs bestowing life.

Kontakion: Though Thou didst descend even into the tomb, O Immortal One, / yet didst Thou destroy the power of hades, / and didst rise as victor, O Christ God, / proclaiming to the myrrh-bearing women: Rejoice! / bestowing peace upon Thine apostles, / and granting ressurrection to the fallen.
