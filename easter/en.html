The Holy Pascha

Troparion: Christ is risen from the dead, / trampling down death by death, / and upon those in the tombs bestowing life.

Kontakion: Though Thou didst descend into the grave, O Immortal One, / yet didst Thou destroy the power of Hades, / and didst arise as victory, O Christ God, / calling to the myrrh-bearing women: Rejoice! / and giving peace unto Thine apostles, / Thou Who dost grant resurrection to the fallen.
