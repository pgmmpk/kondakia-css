The Entrance of the Lord into Jerusalem

Troparion: By raising Lazarus from the dead before Thy passion, / Thou didst confirm the universal resurrection, O Christ God! / Like the children with the branches of victory, / we cry out to Thee, O Vanquisher of Death: / Hosanna in the highest! / Blessed is he that comes in the name of the Lord!

Kontakion: Sitting on Thy throne in heaven, / and carried on a foal on earth, O Christ God, / accept the praise of angels / and the songs of children who sing: / Blessed is he who comes to recall Adam!

Magnification We magnify Thee, / O Christ, Giver of Life, / and we sing to thee, / Hosanna in the highest. / Blessed is He that cometh / in the name of the Lord.
