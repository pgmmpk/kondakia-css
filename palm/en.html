The Entrance of the Lord into Jerusalem

Troparion: Giving us before Thy Passion an assurance of the general resurrection, / Though hast raised Lazarus from the dead, O Christ our God. / Therefore, like the children, we also carry tokens of victory, / and cry to Thee, the Conquerer of death: / Hosanna in the highest; / blessed is He that comes in the name of the Lord.

Kontakion: Seated in heaven upon Thy throne / and on earth upon a foal, O Christ our God, / Though hast accepted the praise of the angels / and the songs of the children who cried out to Thee: / Blessed art Though that comest to call back Adam.

Magnification We magnify Thee, / O Christ, the Giver of Life: / Hosanna in the highest! / And we also cry to Thee, / Blessed is He who comes / in the Name of the Lord.
