St. Olga

Troparion: Having furnished thy mind with wings of divine knowledge, / thou didst soar far above visible creation; / and seeking God, the Creator of all, and finding Him, / thou didst receive rebirth through baptism; / and delighting in the Tree of life, O ever-glorious Olga, / thou remainest incorrupt forever.

Kontakion: Today the grace of the God of all hath appeared, / which glorified the divinely wise Olga in Russia. / Through her supplications, O Lord, / grant remission of sins unto Thy people.

Magnification We magnify thee, / O blessed Princess Olga, equal of the apostles, / and we honor the holy memory of thee / who trampled the idols underfoot / and enlightened many of the people of Russia with holy baptism.
